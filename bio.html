// about

Dr. Joao P. J. Prioli is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering at North Carolina A&T State University, where he directs the Smart Manufacturing & Disassembly Lab (SMDL). His research focuses on closing the loop between product design and manufacturability by modeling and interconnecting heterogeneous and hybrid systems.

His work spans smart manufacturing, automated disassembly, and the development of computational frameworks that integrate data across the product lifecycle — from design to end-of-life processing. Dr. Prioli is committed to advancing sustainable manufacturing through rigorous scientific inquiry and interdisciplinary collaboration.
